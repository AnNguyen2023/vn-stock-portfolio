Invest Journal provides a comprehensive suite of tools for the modern Vietnamese stock investor.

## 1. Portfolio Management
- **Unified Dashboard**: View all holdings, total NAV, and cash balance in one glance.
- **Weighted Average Cost**: Accurate calculation of P/L based on weighted average price.
- **Support for All Exchanges**: Integration for HOSE, HNX, and UPCOM.

## 2. Real-time Market Intelligence
- **Live Ticker**: Prices update every 30 seconds via VPS websocket feedback.
- **Interactive Charts**: Visual history of ticker performance and portfolio growth.
- **Index Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of VN-Index, HNX-Index, and VN30.

## 3. Advanced Performance Analytics
- **Performance Heatmap**: Daily and monthly performance breakdown.
- **YTD Analytics**: Track your portfolio since the beginning of the year.
- **Benchmark Comparison**: (Upcoming) Compare your performance against VN-Index.

## 4. Audit & Journals
- **Transaction History**: Every buy, sell, deposit, and withdrawal is recorded.
- **Daily Snapshots**: Automated logging of EOD (End of Day) portfolio value.
- **Audit Logs**: Traceable history of manual adjustments and system events.

## 5. Security & Privacy
- **Privacy Mode**: One-click to mask sensitive financial figures (NAV, Profit) when using the app in public.
- **JWT Authentication**: Secure login and session management powered by Supabase/PostgreSQL.
- **Rate Limiting**: Protection against API abuse via Redis.
